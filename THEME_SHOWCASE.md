## Pro Designer Tips

1. **Contrast Check**
   - All themes have been tested for WCAG AA contrast
   - Text remains readable on all backgrounds

2. **Color Psychology**
   - Blue = Trust, Professionalism
   - Purple = Creativity, Luxury
   - Green = Growth, Health
   - Orange/Red = Energy, Passion
   - Gold = Wealth, Premium

3. **Brand Alignment**
   - Choose a theme that matches your personal brand
   - Consider your industry standards
   - Test with your profile photo

4. **Accessibility**
   - All themes work for color-blind users
   - Sufficient contrast ratios
   - Clear text hierarchy
